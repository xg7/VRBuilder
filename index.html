    Troop Builder - Customize and Share!

    Welcome to your troop-building companion!

    Whether you're strategizing for battle or perfecting your heroes' loadouts, this Troops Manager gives you the option to:

    Organize Hero Builds

    Add heroes, skills, equipment, and mount skills to create the ultimate troop setups.

    Mix and match up to 5 troops per build for maximum flexibility.

    Share Your Best Loadouts

    Generate a unique shareable URL for each build, no login required!

    Let friends and tribe mates copy your setups with a single click.

    Compare different skill and gear combinations.

    Plan your upgrades and look at heroes and skills that will become available for newer servers.

    Why Use This Tool?

    Manage everything in one place.
    Instant Sharing - Copy, paste, and send your builds in seconds.
    Always Evolving - New features based on player feedback!

    Perfect For:

    Tribe leaders sharing meta builds with members.
    Casual gamers who love experimenting with different setups.

    Further Develepment

    I'm constantly thinking of ways to improve the troop building experience and information that it can give you. But suggestions are always welcome. Furthermore if you see hero skill or talent descriptions with ?? or ??? in it. It's because I don't have the full overview of that particular hero. So if you do have that hero maxed I'd appreciate it if you can provide me with screenshots of that hero's skills and talents. You can find me in the official Viking Rise discord with the name Wimpo. Tag me or send me a DM if you like to help me out. Also if you like me to add a link to your youtube channel, guide, etc..., send me the link i'll add it to the list.

    Dark's Calculator

    Dark made a calculator in google sheets to calculate troop damage output in different situations. It's the best method available to calculate the different outcomes. It's a bit of a learning curve to get it working, but the PVP thread in the official Viking Rise discord can help with that. Also most of the information that you need to use it is also included in the sheets itself.

    Viking Rise Official Discord

    Viking Rise's Official Discord with different language channels, information on new upcoming events, game updates, sections for guides, relevant game topics and of course memes.

    Dark's PVP Guide thread

    Dark is like a math wizard. He made a google spreadsheet calculator which can be used to make troop builds like we can here. Although his calculator is much more in depth with troop damage output etc... This thread is made to discuss troop formations, where his calculator can help a lot by showing the output of those troops. But not only Dark is helpfull in this thread, there are quite a few other players that are willing to give advise on troop builds.

    Bloodcrow's Mount skill upgrading thread

    Bloodcrow is one of those players that is also really helpfull, he made a guide about upgrading mount skills that you should check out! He's also active in Dark's PVP Guide thread. And check out his Youtube channel, link is included on the left under youtube (scroll down).

    Pied Piper's Equipment for Heroes

    Pied Piper made some easy to understand pictures of hero/equipment combo's and what you need to build the equipment. Sadly he never got to it to do infantry. And he seemingly quit playing before Blessed Equipment was introduced. Nonetheless it's still valid for what it offers, and worth checking out if you play on a younger server!

    Gatetrekker's Hero-Gatherers Guide

    Gatetrekker took the time to make an easy to understand overview of gathering heroes, and what those heroes are best used for including the skills you should use for them.

    P1erce's Official Discord

    P1erce is the king of 186, and a youtube streamer. His channel is worth checking out, but so is his discord.

    Bloodcrow's Youtube Channel

    Bloodcrow's Youtube channel has some nice video's of how to tackle some of the different game modes in viking rise. Like Hero Reclamation, Arena and Battle of the Ruins. There's also some videos of Law of the Jungle, a game mode no longer available in the game, but it can still be usefull choosing skills for your heroes.

    P1erce's Youtube Channel

    P1erce's (king of 186) Youtube channel Has some nice troop building guides, interviews with other players from the game and live streams from time to time. An overall interesting channel and interesting character to follow.
